About Me

Hi! My name is Dylan Chatterjea and I am a third-year Computational Media major with threads in Interaction Design and People attending Georgia Tech. I've been doing graphic design for 7 years where I've been able to certify in multiple design applications, work on multiple different design products, and hopefully continue and become a well expereinced designer. In the future I hope to pursue a career in UI/UX design or Digital Marketing, sincee I feel that would be the best place where I can work creatively and use my designs too spread my passion across the world. In my free time, I enjoy playing sports, drawing, and spending time with friends. I strive to balance my academic work with my love for design, always aiming to grow both professionally and personally.
